Records

Through out his career Harry Styles has released 3 studio solo albums.

Album: 1

Self Titled: Harry Styles

Released May 12th 2017, Harry's first album includes 10 songs. Popular singles from the album include "Sign of the Times" and "Kiwi".

Album: 2

Fine Line

Released December 13th 2019, Harry's second album includes 12 songs. Popular singles from the album include "Watermelon Sugar", "Adore You" and "Fine Line".

Album: 3

Harry's House

Released May 20th 2022, Harry's third album includes 13 songs. Popular singles from the album include "Late Nigth Talking", "As it Was" and "Daylight".
